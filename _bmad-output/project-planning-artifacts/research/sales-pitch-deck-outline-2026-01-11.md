## Presentation Tips

### Before the Meeting

- [ ] Research their current platform (if any)
- [ ] Know their event types
- [ ] Prepare custom pricing comparison
- [ ] Have demo account ready

### During the Pitch

- [ ] Ask about their pain points first (Slide 2)
- [ ] Show the pricing math with THEIR numbers (Slide 6)
- [ ] Demo the AI insights live (Slide 8)
- [ ] Offer to set up their account together (Slide 12)

## Design Notes

### Visual Style

- **Colors:** Tunisian-inspired palette (consider national colors + modern accent)
- **Typography:** Clean, readable, bilingual-ready (Arabic/French/English)
- **Imagery:** Real Tunisian events, diverse event types
- **Icons:** Consistent, modern, professional

### Slide Design Principles

1. **One idea per slide** - Don't overcrowd
2. **Visual > Text** - Show, don't tell
3. **Numbers stand out** - Make stats prominent
4. **Competitor comparison** - Use tables for clarity
5. **Screenshots** - Real product, not mockups

### Bilingual Considerations

- Prepare Arabic version for Arabic-first prospects
- All screenshots should show Arabic UI option
- Presenter should be comfortable in both languages
